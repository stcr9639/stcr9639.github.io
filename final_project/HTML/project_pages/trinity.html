Aims of the Project

  • Aim1: Develop a LDEM-specific, multi-environment virtual training simulator.
  • Aim 2: Quantify brain activation during training to assess the environment's efficacy as a countermeasure to neural decrements in spaceflight.
  • Aim 3: Investigate use of the virtual training environment for performance maintenance.
  • Aim 4: Demonstrate long-term acceptability, engagement, and skills transfer in an isolated, confined, controlled environment (ICC).
