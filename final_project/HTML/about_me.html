About Me

Hello! My name is Stella Cross. I'm a fourth year student at CU Boulder. I am a double major in aerospace engineering and chemistry with a minor in creative technology & design. I'm interested in bioastronautics and supporting the next generation of astronauts. My passion for space started back in high school when I participated in Western Aerospace Scholars (WAS). WAS is a program put on by the Museum of Flight and Boeing to teach high schoolers physics, orbital dynamics, technical writing, and engineering skills over a 2-year period.

In my time at CU Boulder, I've learned many coding languages from MATLAB to HTML. My wide-array has lead to a diverse portfolio of projects that I've worked on. Feel free to check out the 'portfolio' section of the website to get a feel for the vast array of projects!
